The Problems We Saw

📈 High Barriers to Entry

Setting up grid bots on crypto exchanges requires technical knowledge and significant capital, putting it beyond reach of amateur traders.

👥 Account Management Limitations

Lead traders cannot manage multiple client accounts effectively on trading bots.

⚙️ No Mass Deployment

Impossible for lead traders to deploy trading bots on thousands of individual accounts simultaneously.

💰 Profit Sharing Trust Issues

After profits are made, clients could default on profit-sharing agreements since all funds are under their control.

🛑 Client Interference

Amateur traders could disrupt active trading, abort positions, or withdraw funds without the expert trader's consent.

The NodeVT Solution

🔗 Unified Trading

The front end captures users' crypto positions and replicates them exactly on Binance trading bot.

💰 Pooled Resources

Smaller deposits are combined to activate bots on the central wallet system on Binance.

🔄 Position Perming

Similar crypto positions are grouped and activated together on the bot for efficiency.

📊 Transparent Distribution

Real results from central wallets are transmitted back to NodeVT software, displaying individual yield based on holdings and market performance.

🎯 Mission

To democratize algorithmic trading by providing retail investors with transparent, accessible tools that were once only available to institutions, while promoting DeFi adoption across Africa and beyond.

👁️ Vision

A future where anyone, anywhere, can participate in financial markets with sophisticated trading tools that mimic dividend systems in global stock markets, giving more power to Decentralized Finance.
